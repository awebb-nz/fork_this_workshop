# Alex Webb

Alex Webb is a 34-year-old librarian who lives in a mid-sized town in the Midwest. He spends most of his days cataloguing new arrivals, helping patrons find obscure reference books, and quietly reshelving whatever people leave on the tables.

Outside of work, Alex enjoys crossword puzzles, tending to a small herb garden on his apartment balcony, and watching documentaries about architecture. He drives a sensible grey hatchback that he bought secondhand six years ago and has never had any real trouble with.

Alex is generally considered reliable and pleasant, though not especially memorable. He remembers the names of regular library visitors and always returns borrowed items on time.
